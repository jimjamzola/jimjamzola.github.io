ASCEND Convening

A small, focused gathering of ASCEND fellows and invited mentors to write, plan, and build next steps. Arizona State University (Tempe campus).

Dates & basic flow: single-day convening with morning talks, writing workshops, and afternoon report-out. Exact room assignments and final logistics will be emailed to registered attendees.
Supported by: Alliance of Hispanic Serving Research Universities
Generous support from: The Luce Foundation
